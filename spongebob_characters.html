Characters

SpongeBob has a lot of friends!

It's important to learn about each character because they all help see the bigger picture in this show.

Patrick Star

Patrick is SpongeBob's best friend who lives two houses away. Patrick is a starfish who lives under a rock. He considers himself to be intelligent, but is unaware of his own stupidity and ignorance.

Squidward Tentacles

Squidward is SpongeBob's next-door neighbor and coworker at the Krusty Krab. He's an octopus who is often seen being grumpy and mean. He hates his job as a cashier and loves playing the clarinet. He is always annoyed by SpongeBob.

Mr. Krabs

Mr. Krabs is SpongeBob's boss who is incredibly greedy. He's a red crab and is the owner of the Krusty Krab. He's a father figure to SpongeBob, and is an actual father to his teenage daughter who's a sperm whale named Pearl.

Sandy Cheeks

Sandy is a squirrel with a Southern accent who wears an air-filled diving suit to breathe underwater. She lives in a clear glass dome to protect herself from the water. She's a thrill-seeking squirrel who is incredibly energetic.
