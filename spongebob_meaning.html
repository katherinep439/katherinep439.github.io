About SpongeBob

SpongeBob SquarePants is an American animated comedy series created by marine science and educator Stephen Hillenburg. It officially aired on July 17th, 1999 on Nickelodeon. SpongeBob is the fourth longest-running American animated series in history, and the longest-running American children's animated series as of 2025. This alone should be enough to show the greatness of SpongeBob.

The show follows SpongeBob SquarePants who's a sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under ths sea. He is incredibly optimistic and joyful which is seen in most, if not all, parts of his life. He works as a fry cook in a fast food restaurant called the Krusty Krab. SpongeBob loves to catch jellyfish, blow bubbles, and is on obtaining a boat-driving license from Mrs. Puff's Boating School. Although recently he did obtain a license, he decided he was not in fact ready for the responsibilities of the road.
